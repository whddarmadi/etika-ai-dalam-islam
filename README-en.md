## 📖 About This Book

**AI Ethics in Islam** is a free and open pocket guide on how a Muslim should view, use, and build artificial intelligence — grounded in the guidance of the Qur'an and the Sunnah.

This book was born from the unease of an AI practitioner who asked: *"Is everything I do with AI in keeping with the guidance of Islamic law?"*

### What it covers:
- May AI depict human beings?
- What is the ruling on a dataset containing unlawful content?
- The danger of deepfakes in the Islamic perspective
- Hidden pitfalls in everyday AI use
- Continuous sin vs. continuous charity in the digital age
- A vision of Islamic civilization in the face of AI
